Help

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about filing and tracking grievances.

View All FAQs ‚Üí
Who can file a grievance?

Any citizen of India can file a grievance related to government services or public issues.

How long does resolution take?

Most grievances are resolved within 30 working days. You'll receive status updates via SMS.

Can I file anonymously?

Mobile number verification is required for accountability and to notify you of updates.

What if I'm not satisfied with the resolution?

You can send a reminder or escalate the grievance to higher authorities via our portal.
