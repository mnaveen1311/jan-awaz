Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about filing and tracking grievances.

üìù Filing a Grievance

Who can file a grievance? +

Any citizen of India can file a grievance related to government services, public facilities, or administrative issues. You only need a valid mobile number to register.

What types of grievances are accepted? +

We accept grievances related to all government departments including Health, Education, PWD, Revenue, Police, Electricity, Water Supply, and more. RTI matters, court-pending cases, and service matters of government employees (without exhausting departmental options) are not accepted.

How do I file a grievance? +

Visit the "File Grievance" section, verify your mobile number via OTP, fill in the details about your complaint, and submit. You'll receive a Reference ID immediately after submission.

üîç Tracking & Status

How long does resolution take? +

Most grievances are resolved within 30 working days. Complex cases may take longer. You'll receive SMS notifications at every stage of the process.

How do I track my grievance? +

You can track using your Reference ID or registered mobile number from the "Track Status" page. You can view the complete timeline of actions taken.

What if my grievance is not resolved in time? +

You can send a reminder from the "Send Reminder" section. If the issue persists, you can escalate it to higher authorities through our portal. Repeated escalations are reviewed by senior officers.

üîí Privacy & Security

Is my personal information safe? +

Yes. Your personal information is encrypted and stored securely. It is only used to process your grievance and send you status updates. We do not share your data with third parties.

Can I file anonymously? +

Mobile verification is required for accountability purposes and to notify you of updates. However, your contact details are not shared with the respondent department in most cases.
